Timings and Branches

The Morning Shift operates from 7:30 AM to 12:10 PM, exclusively for female higher secondary students. The Day Shift is for male students at the higher secondary level, and both male and female students at the undergraduate level. The Professional BBA course is available for both male and female students.

Prayer Facilities

The college has its own mosque, with a separate prayer area designated for female students.

Library and Seminar Facilities

The college boasts a well-equipped library with nearly 10,000 books. Each honors department has a dedicated seminar hall. Practical labs are well-equipped, and practical classes are mandatory once a week for each subject.

Student and Staff Strength

The total number of students is approximately 8,000, with 120 teachers. Additionally, there are 70 education support staff from Class III and IV.

Infrastructure

The college is located on a 1.5-acre plot with four buildings: - Independence Building (10 stories) - Victory Building (5 stories) - Ekushey Building (5 stories) - Information Technology Building (8 stories) There are 150 rooms and 8 labs across the campus.

Online Presence

Dhania College has an official website, though it is currently inactive due to domain registration issues. The college maintains an active Facebook page and 13 departmental Facebook groups. There are separate Facebook groups for the Science, Humanities, and Business branches of the higher secondary level. The college also has a Facebook Archive where past activities are stored.

Online Classes and Video Resources

The college has an **official YouTube channel** where important class videos are uploaded for students. Online class videos and other activity videos are archived and can be accessed by students through provided links.
